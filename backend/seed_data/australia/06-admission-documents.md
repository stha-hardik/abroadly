# Australia Admission Document Checklist

The training documents and admission workbook repeated the same core admission checklist across many providers. Use this checklist for offer/admission stage before CoE and visa.

## Core Admission Documents

- Passport biodata page. Some providers request all passport pages or front/back scans.
- Academic documents from SEE/SLC and Class 12 for undergraduate or pathway applications.
- Bachelor's transcripts, provisional certificate, character certificate, completion letter, and degree certificate for master's applications.
- English test result: IELTS, PTE Academic, TOEFL iBT, Cambridge, OET, or provider-approved alternative.
- Statement of Purpose or Genuine Student-style questionnaire if the provider requests one.
- Updated CV/resume for master's, research, gap, or work-experience cases.
- Work experience letters, training certificates, or internship evidence where relevant.
- Gap explanation and supporting proof if there is a study gap, usually especially after 6 months.
- Letters of recommendation for many master's, research, health, social work, or higher-tier applications.
- Marriage certificate and spouse documents if marital status is relevant to the application or visa plan.
- Application form, portfolio, research proposal, or supplementary questionnaire if required by the provider.

## Document Quality Rules

Scans should be clear, complete, uncropped, and readable. Names and dates must match across passport, transcripts, English test, citizenship, marriage certificate, and bank documents. If names differ, the student may need an affidavit or official correction.

English translations are needed if a document is not in English. Use a certified translator or official issuing body where possible.

## Academic Documents by Level

For Class 12 to bachelor:

- SEE/SLC marksheet and certificate if requested.
- NEB Class 11 and Class 12 transcripts/marksheets.
- Character certificate and provisional/migration certificate if available.
- Subject prerequisites for IT, engineering, health, science, teaching, and nursing.

For bachelor to master:

- All semester/year transcripts.
- Provisional certificate or completion letter if final certificate is pending.
- Degree certificate if issued.
- Grading scale or backlogs/attempt certificate if provider asks.
- CV, SOP, LOR, and work certificates when relevant.

## Admission Outcome Terms

- Offer letter: provider has assessed the student and offered a place, often conditional.
- Conditional offer: student must still meet conditions such as final transcript, English score, deposit, GS form, or documents.
- Unconditional offer: academic/admission conditions are met, but visa and CoE steps remain.
- Acceptance agreement: student accepts the offer and agrees to terms.
- CoE: Confirmation of Enrolment, issued after offer acceptance and required payments/OSHC steps. CoE is required for the Student visa.

## Follow-Up Questions

- Is this for offer/admission, CoE, NOC, or visa?
- Which provider and course?
- Is the offer conditional or unconditional?
- Do you have final transcripts or only provisional results?
- Is there a study gap, backlog, marriage, spouse, or dependent child?
