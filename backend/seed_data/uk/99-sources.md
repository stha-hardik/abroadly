## Domains the corpus deliberately does NOT cite

Per the working rules (see AGENTS.md), the corpus does not cite:

- Consultancy blogs (IDP, Yocket, Leverage Edu, etc.)
- Reddit / Quora threads
- Forum scraping
- Affiliate-marketing study-abroad sites
- WhatsApp group screenshots
- Personal blogs

Anyone editing this corpus: please match this discipline. The product's trust contract depends on it.
